Frequently Asked Questions

What is an entity?

An entity is a single company or legal body whose cap table you manage. Professional and Business plans include 1 entity. Enterprise supports multiple entities with separate cap tables, registries, and permissions.

Can I upgrade later?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate your billing accordingly.

Do you support multiple stock classes?

Absolutely. AegisIQ supports Common, Preferred, Warrants, and Options with detailed series and class-level granularity across all plans.

Is the ledger auditable?

Yes. Every action is logged with timestamps, user attribution, and immutable records. Business and Enterprise plans include audit snapshot exports.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes! Annual billing saves you 20%. Toggle the pricing switch above to see annual rates.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.
