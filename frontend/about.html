Our Mission

To democratize access to institutional-grade equity management tools, enabling every company to manage their cap table with the same sophistication as Fortune 500 firms.

AegisIQ was founded with a simple belief: managing equity shouldn't be complicated. Whether you're a startup issuing your first stock options or an enterprise fund managing hundreds of portfolio companies, you deserve tools that are powerful yet intuitive.

We've built AegisIQ Stock Ledger to be the definitive platform for cap table management, combining enterprise-grade security with a user experience that anyone can master.
